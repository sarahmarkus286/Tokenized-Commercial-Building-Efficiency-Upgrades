# Tokenized Commercial Building Efficiency Upgrades

## Overview
This project implements a blockchain-based system for managing commercial building energy efficiency upgrades through tokenization. By creating digital representations of buildings, improvement projects, and energy performance, this solution enables transparent tracking of efficiency initiatives, contractor qualifications, and realized energy savings.

## Core Components

### Property Registration Contract
- Creates digital twins of commercial buildings with unique identifiers
- Records essential building details including size, age, construction type, and systems
- Maintains ownership history and property modifications
- Stores baseline energy consumption data and efficiency ratings
- Enables property tokenization for tracking improvements and value changes

### Improvement Project Contract
- Defines specific energy efficiency measures and interventions
- Creates smart project plans with milestones and deliverables
- Manages funding allocation and disbursement for approved upgrades
- Tracks material specifications and installation requirements
- Implements verification protocols for completed improvements

### Contractor Verification Contract
- Validates qualified service providers for efficiency projects
- Manages certification records and specialized qualifications
- Verifies contractor insurance, bonding, and regulatory compliance
- Tracks contractor performance history and quality metrics
- Ensures appropriate expertise matches for specific improvement types

### Performance Tracking Contract
- Monitors energy consumption and savings after completed upgrades
- Records performance data with secure oracle integrations
- Compares actual savings against projected improvements
- Generates verification reports for energy efficiency incentives
- Provides tokenized representation of achieved efficiency gains

## Benefits
- Creates transparent records of building improvements for valuation
- Streamlines verification for utility rebates and tax incentives
- Enables efficiency performance tracking for ESG reporting
- Reduces friction in financing energy improvement projects
- Builds credibility for green building certifications and standards
